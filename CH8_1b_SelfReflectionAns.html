Ch 8.1b Circuit Diagrams (電路圖)

Q1. Battery Symbol (電池組符號)
Which symbol represents a 'Battery' (more than one cell)?
哪一個符號代表「電池組」(多於一粒電芯)？ Answer: (B)
Why? 解釋：
• (A) ---| |--- is a Cell (電芯).
• (B) ---| |---| |--- shows multiple cells connected, which is a Battery (電池組).
• (C) ---X--- usually represents a load or bulb (depending on convention).
Q2. Drawing Wires (繪畫導線)
True or False: In a circuit diagram, wires can be drawn as curved lines.
是非題：在電路圖中，導線可以畫成曲線。 Answer: False (錯)
Rule 規則：
Wires must always be drawn as straight lines using a ruler. They should turn at right angles (90°).
導線必須用直尺畫成直線，轉彎處應呈直角 (90°)。
