Ch 8.1 Diagnostic Answers

自學診斷 - 參考答案

Question 1 (Foundation / 基礎)
Which group of materials are ALL 'conductors'?
下列哪一組物料全都是「導電體」？
Answer 答案： (B)
Iron nail (Metal) & Graphite (Pencil lead) are both conductors.
鐵釘(金屬)和石墨(鉛筆芯)都是導電體。
Question 2 (Advanced / 進階)
List two possible causes for an 'Open Circuit'.
請列出兩個可能造成「斷路」的原因。
Suggested Answers 參考答案：
  • Battery has no energy.
    電池沒電。
  • Wire is broken or loose connection.
    電線斷了或連接鬆脫。
  • Switch is open (off).
    開關未閉合。
  • Bulb is broken.
    燈泡壞了。
(Any two of the above / 以上任選其二)
Question 3 (Application / 應用)
Why is copper used inside wires, while the outside is covered with plastic?
為什麼電線的內部要用銅，而外部要包著一層塑膠？
Answer 答案：

Inside (Copper): It is a conductor to allow electricity to flow.
內部 (銅)：它是導電體，讓電流通過。

Outside (Plastic): It is an insulator to prevent electric shock.
外部 (塑料)：它是絕緣體，防止觸電。
