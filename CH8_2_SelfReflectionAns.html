Ch 8.2 Diagnostic Answers

Current & Its Effects (電流及其效應)

Question 1 Unit (單位)
The unit of current is _______.
電流的單位是 _______。
✅ Answer: (B) Ampere 安培 (A)
Explanation 解釋
  • (A) Volt (V) is for Voltage (電壓).
  • (C) Watt (W) is for Power (功率).
  • Ampere (A) is the correct unit for measuring flow of charges.
Question 2 Connection (連接)
Ammeter must be connected in _______.
安培計必須 _______ 連接。
✅ Answer: (A) Series (串聯)
Why Series? 為什麼是串聯？ An ammeter counts how many charges flow THROUGH it (like a toll gate).
安培計像一個「收費站」，必須安裝在路中間 (串聯) 才能計算流量。
*Warning: Connecting in Parallel causes a Short Circuit! (並聯會導致短路！)
Question 3 Effect (效應)
An Electric Lock uses ______ effect.
磁力鎖利用了 ______ 效應。
✅ Answer: (B) Magnetic (磁)
Concept 概念 When current flows, the coil becomes an Electromagnet (電磁鐵) and attracts the lock bolt.
當電流通過，線圈變成電磁鐵，將鎖舌吸開。
