Ch 8.4 Diagnostic Answers

Resistance & Factors (電阻及其影響因素)

Q1. Factors (因素)
Which wire has the HIGHER Resistance? (哪條電阻較大？)
✅ Answer: (B) Long & Thin (長而幼)
Concept Think of a tunnel:
• Longer = Harder to walk through (More Resistance).
• Thinner = Harder to squeeze through (More Resistance).
想像一條隧道：愈長、愈窄，就愈難通過（電阻愈大）。
Q2. Effect (效果)
To make the bulb DIMMER (變暗), we should...
✅ Answer: (B) Increase resistance (增加電阻)
Logic Higher Resistance (更塞車) ➔ Less Current (車流量減少) ➔ Bulb Dims (燈變暗).
Q3. Unit (單位)
Unit of Resistance is... (電阻的單位是...)
✅ Answer: (B) Ohm (Ω)
Recall • (A) Ampere = Current (電流)
• (C) Volt = Voltage (電壓)
• Ohm (Ω) = Resistance (電阻)
